# Non-Disclosure & Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement

> ⚠️ **Not legal advice.** This is a practical template to get you 90% of the way there.
> Before relying on it, have a qualified lawyer (ideally one familiar with Nigerian law and
> IT contracts) review and adapt it to your situation. Fill in every **[bracketed]** field.

**This Agreement** is made on **[date]** between:

**(1) Owner:** Sterlin Glams, represented by **[Owner full legal name]**, of **[address]**
(the **"Owner"**); and

**(2) Recipient:** **[Full name of developer / contractor / employee / company]**, of
**[address]** (the **"Recipient"**).

The Owner and the Recipient are each a "Party" and together the "Parties".

## 1. Background
The Owner owns a proprietary software platform and business known as **Sterlin Glams**,
including its source code, designs, databases, documentation, and related materials (the
**"Software"**). The Recipient will be given access to the Software and/or will perform work
relating to it. This Agreement protects the Owner's confidential information and secures the
Owner's ownership of all resulting intellectual property.

## 2. Definitions
- **"Confidential Information"** means all non-public information the Recipient obtains from or
  about the Owner or the Software, in any form, including source code, designs, algorithms,
  data models, database contents, customer and supplier data, pricing, business plans,
  credentials, and this Agreement itself.
- **"Work Product"** means everything the Recipient conceives, creates, writes, or contributes
  in connection with the Software or the Owner's business — including code, designs, documents,
  ideas, and improvements — whether alone or with others, and whether or not during working hours.

## 3. Confidentiality
The Recipient shall:
- keep all Confidential Information **strictly confidential** and not disclose it to any third
  party without the Owner's prior written consent;
- use Confidential Information **solely** to perform authorised work for the Owner, and for no
  other purpose;
- protect it with at least reasonable security, and not store or transmit it through unauthorised
  systems or accounts.

## 4. Ownership & Assignment of Intellectual Property
- All **Work Product is created as "work made for hire"** for the Owner and is the Owner's
  exclusive property from the moment it is created.
- To the extent any Work Product does not automatically vest in the Owner, the Recipient
  **irrevocably assigns to the Owner all right, title, and interest** in it worldwide, including
  all copyright, trade secret, patent, and other intellectual-property rights.
- The Recipient **waives any moral rights** in the Work Product to the extent permitted by law and
  agrees to **assist the Owner** (at the Owner's expense) in registering, perfecting, and enforcing
  the Owner's rights, including signing any documents the Owner reasonably requests.
- The Recipient retains **no rights** to use, reuse, or license the Work Product or the Software.

## 5. Restrictions — No Use, Copy, or Competing Product
The Recipient shall **not**, during or after their engagement, without the Owner's prior written
permission:
- use, run, host, or deploy the Software or any part of it for any purpose other than authorised
  work for the Owner;
- copy, clone, fork, download, or retain the Software or Confidential Information;
- build, assist, or advise on any product or service that is derived from, or that copies or
  substantially reproduces, the Software; or
- reverse engineer or attempt to reconstruct the Software from memory, notes, or fragments.

## 6. Return & Deletion
On the Owner's request, or when the Recipient's engagement ends, the Recipient shall immediately
stop all use and **permanently delete or return every copy** of the Software and Confidential
Information in their possession or control, and, if asked, **certify in writing** that they have
done so. The Recipient shall also return or delete any access credentials and remove their access
to the Owner's repositories, servers, and accounts.

## 7. Security Obligations
The Recipient shall follow the Owner's reasonable security instructions, use only Owner-approved
accounts and devices for access where required, keep credentials secret, and report any suspected
leak or unauthorised access to the Owner without delay.

## 8. Term & Survival
This Agreement takes effect on the date above and continues for as long as the Recipient has
access to the Software. The confidentiality, IP-assignment, and restriction obligations
(Sections 3–6) **survive indefinitely** after the engagement ends.

## 9. Remedies
The Recipient agrees that a breach of this Agreement could cause the Owner irreparable harm for
which money damages alone would be inadequate, and that the Owner is entitled to seek **injunctive
relief** in addition to any other remedies available at law or in equity. If the Owner has to
enforce this Agreement, the Recipient shall reimburse the Owner's reasonable legal costs.

## 10. General
- **Governing law & jurisdiction:** This Agreement is governed by the laws of **[Nigeria / your
  state]**, and the Parties submit to the courts of **[jurisdiction]**.
- **Entire agreement:** This is the entire agreement between the Parties on its subject and
  supersedes any prior understanding.
- **Severability:** If any provision is unenforceable, the rest remains in force.
- **No waiver:** Failure to enforce any term is not a waiver of it.
- **Assignment:** The Owner may assign this Agreement; the Recipient may not.
